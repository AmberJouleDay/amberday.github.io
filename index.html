About Me

I am currently a doctoral student at the University of Texas at Austin in the Statistics and Data Sciences department.

I have taught a variety of mathematics courses ranging from middle school and high school up to college level courses. The classes I have taught included diverse student populations and I pride myself on being able to maintain good relationships with my students and foster open communication in my classrooms. Throughout my teaching career I have sought out feedback from my supervisors and students on how I can improve as a teacher. This has helped me identify ways to further advance my teaching techniques and how to help students in more effective ways.
